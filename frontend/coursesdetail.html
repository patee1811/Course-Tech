Description

Are you ready to level up and gain hands-on experience in cybersecurity? Introducing The Complete Cybersecurity Analyst Course—your one-stop solution for mastering the skills and gaining hands-on experience you need to excel in the cybersecurity field.

Prerequisites
As the name suggests, this is a “hands-on” course. You will be required to install virtualization software on your system. We recommend a Windows host system; however, a Mac will work also, but there are some limitations based on newer Apple Silicon chips.

Our goal is to provide everything into one course that includes a wide range of opportunities to increase your knowledge and gain that critical hands-on experience. We will not break up essential knowledge into multiple, expensive modules; this course gives you everything you need in one comprehensive package at a one-time price.
